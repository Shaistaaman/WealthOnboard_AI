# Product Overview

WealthOnboard AI is a modern banking and financial services application focused on customer onboarding and account management.

## Core Features

- User authentication (login, signup, OTP verification, QR code)
- Multi-step customer onboarding workflow
- KYC verification and document upload
- Customer profile management
- Dashboard with account overview
- Settings and notifications

## User Journey

1. Authentication flow with OTP and QR code verification
2. Comprehensive onboarding process covering:
   - Customer information
   - Employment and financial details
   - KYC verification
   - Document uploads
   - Regulatory compliance
   - Trading permissions
   - Risk appetite assessment
3. Dashboard access with profile and settings management

## Design Philosophy

The app uses a premium aesthetic with burgundy and gold color scheme, emphasizing trust and sophistication in financial services.
